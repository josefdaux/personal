CINEMA ARTS & MOTION PICTURE PRODUCTION EXPERIENCE


Partnering with organizations and individuals to coordinate, develop and produce field production based, narrative storytelling with distinct preproduction, production and post production phases intended for festival viewing, theatrical release and streaming distribution. Executing sound production strategies through a combination of tested experience, smart leadership, calm discernment, astute situational awareness, technical knowledge and creative acuity.

Here are some of the projects that I've previously worked on both onscreen and off...
FEATURE FILM - NINJA BADASS (2020)
Roles: Actor/Featured Performer - “Woohoo Hunter”

The Ninja VIP Super Club is doing a slow drag across the American Midwest, culminating in female sacrifice. When they kidnap a super hot babe that local scumbag Rex has his eyes on - it's up to him to become a ninja to steal her back from them. Then she can have his babies! Join Rex, his armless sensei, a badass ball ripper, a dragon, a gang of puppies, some detached feet, and a mean ninja motherf**** who trains by beating up cows for an experience that'll make you wanna eat egg rolls until you sh** your pants. And yes, there is a reason we’re showing this late at night.

SHORT FILM - THE TABLE READ (2020)
Roles: Supporting Actor - "Vince"

A short subject in the vein of numerous classic whodunits and murder mysteries. A wealthy playwright gathers friends and collaborators at his spacious mansion for a table read of his latest and possibly final project. 

SHORT FILM - A WARM, WELL LIGHTED PLACE (2019)
Roles: Head Gaffer

An adaptation of the 1933 short story by author Ernest Hemingway. This short subject focuses on two waiters discussing an elderly patron who refuses to return home after closing time on a cold, dark night.

SHORT FILM - SHIFT (2018)
Roles: Audio Technician

This Los Angeles Experimental, Dance & Music Festival, Idaho Screendance Festival and Montage Film Festival award winning short subject reimagines mundane, everyday human interactions as an escapist ballet.

SHORT FILM - TIS OF THEE (2018)
Roles: Background Performance/Extra - Audience Member

An honest examination of a man coping with personal regret in public spaces as he faces his inevitable demise. This film explores the fragility and introspection of the current state of democracy through the lens of a love lost. Starring John Robert Armstrong of “The Good Catholic.”

SHORT FILM - DON'T CRY OVER (2005)
Roles: Producer, Director, Writer

Writer/Director/Producer of the 2006 Multivisions Award winner for Creative Achievement in Field Production. This short subject comedy noir is an avant-garde look at loss through the eyes of a broken detective who recounts the events surrounding the loss of his partner.
